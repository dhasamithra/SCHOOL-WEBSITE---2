introduction

welcome to csacademy

The CS Academy schools are K-12 international, day and boarding, co-educational schools with campuses in Erode and Coimbatore in Tamil Nadu, India. The schools offer both CBSE and Cambridge International curricula.The first campus of CS Academy was started in the year 2009 in Erode, Tamil Nadu. CS Academy is named after the late Bharat Ratna awardee, Shri C. Subramaniam (CS). CS Academy is part of the BVB, Erode group of schools, with the management team having more than 45 years' experience in delivering all round excellence in education.The school has an open campus with extensive facilities for all sports and co-curricular activities.



campus life

Co-curricular activities and sports are woven into our curriculum and are compulsory every day. Children from standards I to V are exposed to a wide variety of activities as part of their schedule. From STD VI onwards, students are given a choice to decide what activities they want to focus on. Children who excel in sports are selected to be part of the school team.
